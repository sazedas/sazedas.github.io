Goal Oriented Analysis Model

Overview

Mental health is quickly becoming a worldwide concern of epidemic proportions. UCI has stepped up against these health epidemics by creating the UCI Center for Student Wellness and Health Promotion (CSWHP) (https://studentwellness.uci.eduLinks to an external site.). Their vision is to “strive to be a leader in collegiate health promotion by focusing on the unique and relevant health needs and concerns of UCI students to support their academic success.” As part of this vision, they are commissioning campus teams to propose a technology-based approach (AnteaterHealth) to address the mental and physical health needs of UCI students, faculty, and staff. As a long-term goal, they are hoping to set an example and “pave the way” for other UC schools (and beyond) to follow suit and either use or adapt the technology developed to help meet the health needs of university populations everywhere. CSWHP requests that AnteaterHealth encompass two separate subsystems.1) AntPhysHealth and 2) AntMentalHealth. Our development team developed a goal model based on our understanding of the proposed system - AntMentalHealth, a system that promotes mental health, including discussion with our customer team and stakeholders. This goal model was refined to the granularity of requirements and expectations.
